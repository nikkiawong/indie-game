Eva Moonbean

Same. Typical. Day. Eva is so sick of hearing the same ol bullshit from her gramp gramps, she decides she is leaving home for good.

Gramps gramps has warned her many times... "Never go into the woods behind the house! Never walk the trail marked 'Pacific Crest...'". So, that's exactly where Eva is going today.

With her sidekick squirrel Kwomp, Eva is on a path of adventure and being lost and surrounded by daaaangerous creatures!

Join Eva in all her teenage angst! Battle forest ghosts, and save the berry faeries from the forest demon! And, save yourself...

Kwomp McNutters

No forest adventurer should leave their gramps gramps' house without a faithful companion.

Kwomp is the latest development in modern squirrel technology. Equipped with blade, stunning cape, his IRL identity remains a mystery to all but Eva Moonbean.

Scout ahead with Kwomp, battle foes, fly from tree to tree (he's not a flying squirrel, we just have the technology).

Kwomp and Eva, tour de force, begin their journey in The Way Out!

Forest Denizens

Not all forest creatures are all cuddles and fun fun (though, some are).

With over 2 different types of monsters to battle, The Way Out has at least some level of replayability!

Face off against Snarflats in the Shadowed Cove, race the Zoomeries through the branches of the Evergreen Grove or cuddle up with a friendly Maossss.
